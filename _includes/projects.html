Cash Flow Forecasting — Treasury Analysis

End-to-end treasury analysis applied to the Olist Brazilian E-Commerce dataset (~100k orders). Models payment settlement rules by channel (D+0 to D+30×installment), projects receivables, estimates operational outflow, builds an aging curve and forecasts 30-day cash inflow using Holt-Winters with a 90% confidence interval. Validated via out-of-sample backtesting against a Naive benchmark.
